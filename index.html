About Rádio Jardim

Rádio Jardim is a mobile community radio exploring soundscapes and stories to unite listeners through auditory experiences. Join us in celebrating the power of sound!

Listen Live

Experience a curated selection of soundscapes, music, and community stories. Tune in for our monthly live broadcasts.

Participate

Be part of Rádio Jardim by sharing your soundscapes or music. Record the sounds of your garden or curate a playlist for our community.
